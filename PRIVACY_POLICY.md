# Purple Pickaxe Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 20, 2026

Purple Pickaxe is a browser extension that helps users automatically collect Twitch channel points and claim Twitch Drops.

## Data Collection
Purple Pickaxe does not sell personal information and does not use advertising trackers.

The extension may store the following locally on your device:
- Extension settings and preferences
- Session statistics (if enabled)
- Twitch Drop and channel point tracking information

## Permissions Used
- storage: Saves extension settings and statistics locally.
- cookies: Used only to detect and maintain the user's Twitch session as required for extension functionality.
- tabs: Used to detect when Twitch tabs are open and to interact with Twitch pages.
- https://www.twitch.tv/* and https://gql.twitch.tv/*: Required to read Twitch data and claim points and drops.

## Data Sharing
Purple Pickaxe does not transmit or sell user data to third parties.
